Bioengineering Day

May 19th, 2016 at The BIOCOM Institute


Bioengineering Day, otherwise known as BE Day, is a unique forum for networking between our industrial partners and the bioengineering community at UC San Diego. This undergraduate and graduate research symposia celebrates the accomplishments of our students, and provides opportunities for collaboration between them and fellow members from local industry, department alumni, or academia. BE Day features a keynote speaker, a variety of graduate research presentations, a poster expo, an industry panel discussion, and a networking reception.
