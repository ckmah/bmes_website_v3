Industry & Academia Panels

At the various panels we host throughout the year, graduate students and industry professionals from a variety of backgrounds are able to share their experiences with life beyond the undergraduate degree. At this event, students have the opportunity to connect with participants through a Q&A session! Past participants include M.S. candidates, Ph.D. candidates, and M.D./Ph.D. candidates, as well as industry companies such as Covidian and Illumina
