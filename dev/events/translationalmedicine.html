About

Translational medicine is the conversion of scientific discovery into health improvement, bringing the "bench to bedside" by innovating new cures, integrating these techniques into practice, and inspiring future doctors and scientists.

Translational Medicine Day will bridge the discussions occurring in San Diegoâ€™s vibrant and expansive research community with the future of cutting edge healthcare, putting students, professors, and industry together to enhance the heart of this discussion. The event will have speakers, workshops, research labs, and panels that seek to demonstrate the potential of translational research in medicine and also provide undergraduates a look into opportunities to contribute to the field.
